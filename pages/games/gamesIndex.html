Challenge of the Day

There will be a new challenge question every day (resets at 12:00 AM EST). Players get a score every day out of 100 based on accuracy and time taken to solve the challenge problem. Players' scores are cumulative and players with a public account are ranked in a public leaderboard based on their cumulative score.
